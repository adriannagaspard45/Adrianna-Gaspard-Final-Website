What is 10 Cloverfield Lane About?

After surviving a car accident, Michelle wakes up to find herself in an underground bunker with two men. Howard tells her that a massive chemical attack has rendered the air unbreathable, and their only hope of survival is to remain inside. Despite the comforts of home, Howard's controlling and menacing nature makes Michelle want to escape. After taking matters into her own hands, the young woman finally discovers the truth about the outside world.

Source: Google Film Description

Understanding the Main Character

Michelle is the main protagnoist of 10 Cloverfield Lane. Going through Louisiana, Michelle goes to a gas station to refuel her car and notices a man in a pickup truck watching her. While driving, Michelle is contacted by Ben, her husband, who begs her to come back and to stop running away. After hearing on the radio that a power surge is happening, she is knocked off the road when she is contacted by Ben once again.

Source: hero.fandom.com

A Look at the Rest of the Characters

Howard Stambler, played by John Goodman, is the main antagonist of 10 Cloverfield Lane. He was a former US Sailor who worked with satellites. He and his teammates discovered a terrible secret about the future of the world. After that he became paranoid and began to prepare himself for an unknown threat of some kind. Emmett Dewitt, played by John Gallagher Jr. is one of the main characters. Before the events of the movie, he was hired by Howard to help build his bunker.
