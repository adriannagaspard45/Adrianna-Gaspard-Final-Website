The Craft

"The Craft is truly a great and exciting film. It blends supernatural elements with teenage hardships in an excellent way" Adrianna Gaspard

What is The Craft About?

After transferring to a Los Angeles high school, Sarah finds that her telekinetic gift appeals to a group of three wannabe witches, who happen to be seeking a fourth member for their rituals. Bonnie, Rochelle, and Nancy, like Sarah herself, all have troubled backgrounds, which combined with their nascent powers lead to dangerous consequences. When a minor spell causes a fellow student to lose her hair, the girls grow power-mad.

Understanding the Main Character

Sarah Bailey is a new student at her high school, where she feels like an outsider. She has powers that interests three girls that also go to her school. Sarah first has good time with the girls and enjoys using her powers, but as time goes on, Sarah begins to realize that there's a divide for what she wants to use her powers for, and what the other girls want.

A Look at the Rest of the Characters

Bonnie Harper, played by Neve Campbell, is a main character in The Craft. She is a practicing witch. Bonnie is presented as a shy, insecure teenager who suffers from burn scars along her back and shoulders as a result of a fire when she was a child. Rochelle Zimmerman, played by Rachel True, is a main character in The Craft. She is an aspiring witch who forms a coven with Sarah Bailey, Nancy Downs and Bonnie. She uses magic to get revenge on a classmate who bullies her, but gets more than she bargained for. Nancy Downs is the film's main antagonist. She gains magical abilites after forming a coven with the girls.
