What is The Wedding Planner About?

While celebrating her newest and most lucrative account; the wedding of Internet tycoon Fran Donelly, Mary is rescued from a near-fatal collision with a runaway dumpster by handsome pediatrician Steve Edison. After spending the most enchanting evening of their lives together, Mary thinks she's finally found a reason to believe in love. What she doesn't know is that cupid and her career are about to collide head-on.

Source: Google Film Description

Understanding the Main Character

Mary Fiore, played by Jennifer Lopez, is a dedicated and talented wedding planner known for her meticulous attention to detail and her passion for creating unforgettable experiences. Living in San Franscisco, she thrives on the excitement of orchestrating perfect weddings, often pouring her heart into each event.

Source: Wikipedia

A Look at the Rest of the Characters

Mathhew McConaughey plays Steve Edison, the funny and charming groom of the bride. Penny, played by Judy Greer is Mary's best friend and coworker. Massimo, played by Justin Chambers, is a quirky guy who Mary knew from childhood that her dad wants her to get married to. Salvatore Fiore, played by Alex Rocco is Mary's father. Bridgette Wilson plays Fran Donolly, who is the bride.
