Groundhog Day

Fun facts

  1. Bill Murray was bitten twice by the groundhog while filming.
  2. Tom Hanks was originally considered for the role of Phil, but he was thought to be "too nice."
  3. The film was filmed in Woodstock, Illinois, not Punxsutawney, Pennsylvania.
  4. The film didn't premire on Groundhog day (February 2). It premiered ten days after that on February 12.
  5. Phil's day repeated over 12,000 times, which is equivalent to 33 years.

What is Groundhog Day About?

A cynical TV weatherman finds himself reliving the same day over and over again when he goes on location to the small town of Punxsutawney to film a report about their annual Groundhog Day. His predicament drives him to distraction, until he sees a way of turning the situation to his advantage.

Source: Google Film Description

Understanding the Main Character

Phil Connors, played by Bill Murray, is a jaded and self-absorbed TV weatherman. He's known for his sarcastic demeanor and pessimistic outlook on life. Bill changes his outlook on life after he is forced to go through the same day repeatedly.

Source: Google Film Description

A Look at the Rest of the Characters

Rita, played by Andie MacDowell, is Phil's producer and love interest. She's caring and optimistic. Larry, played by Chris Elliott is Phil's funny cameraman. Ned is Phil's former classmate and a salesman who keeps running into Phil.
